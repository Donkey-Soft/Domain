Optional feature - only if you enable:

Voice Input

Optional - only when you tap microphone:
• Voice audio sent to Google Speech Recognition
• Transcribed text returned to app
• Audio processed transiently (not permanently stored)

User Control: Only activates when you tap the microphone button in journal
Data Sent: Raw audio recording of your voice
Google's Use: May be used to improve speech recognition services
Fallback: If speech recognition unavailable, manual typing always works
Alternative: Type manually instead
Google Privacy: policies.google.com/privacy

Android Auto-Backup

System-level feature (Google controls):
Your Android OS may automatically back up app data to your Google account if enabled in device settings.

What's included: App settings, alarms, sound preferences, journal entries
What's excluded (for security): Journal passwords, backup encryption keys
Disable: Settings > System > Backup
Note: We've configured the app to exclude sensitive data from automatic backups

Your Rights

You can:
✅ Delete all local data (clear app data/uninstall)
✅ Delete Drive backups:
   1. Open Google Drive app or drive.google.com
   2. Navigate to "My Drive"
   3. Look for files ending in ".RestfulBackup"
   4. Delete files and empty trash
✅ Revoke Drive access: myaccount.google.com/permissions
✅ Reset Advertising ID (Settings > Google > Ads)
✅ Opt-out of personalized ads (device settings)

You cannot:
❌ Delete AdMob data (Google controls retention)

Permissions Explained

Why we need these (NOT for tracking):
• SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM - Precise alarm times
• USE_FULL_SCREEN_INTENT - Launch alarm full-screen over lock screen
• DISABLE_KEYGUARD - Dismiss lock screen when alarm rings
• RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED - Restore alarms after device restart
• WAKE_LOCK - Keep device awake during alarm
• VIBRATE - Alarm vibration
• POST_NOTIFICATIONS - Show notifications
• FOREGROUND_SERVICE - Background audio
• READ_MEDIA_AUDIO - Access your custom sounds
• READ_MEDIA_IMAGES - Access custom background images (Android 13+)
• READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE - Access custom sounds/images (Android 12 and below)
• SYSTEM_ALERT_WINDOW - Show alarm over lockscreen
• PERSIST_URI_PERMISSIONS - Remember your custom sound/image selections

Implicit (added by AdMob):
• INTERNET - Load ads
• ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE - Check connectivity
• ACCESS_WIFI_STATE - Network connectivity for ads
